Guarantee

All our products carry a 1 year guarantee for complete peace of mind. Our products are carefully inspected prior to despatch to ensure customer satisfaction. We want you to enjoy your product for many years. To help ensure this, please make sure you regularly maintain your product (as described in your user manual). If you don't have a user manual, please visit the manufacturer's support website (Vax, Hoover, Electrolux etc) to download this for free. If for some reason your product develops a problem, please refer to the user manual for guidance as well as visiting our trouble shooting pages before contacting us, as in 9 out of 10 cases this will normally rectify the problem.
